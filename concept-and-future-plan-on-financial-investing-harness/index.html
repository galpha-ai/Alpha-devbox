An AI researcher neo-lab merging quant and discretionary investing

AlphaDev is a persistent, hosted AI researcher for investment. It takes a trader's view in natural language and turns it into real research — backtest, scenario, small-size live trade, process postmortem — on a shared facts layer a team can compound on.

Backtest any idea. Simulate any scenario.
Automate low-value decisions. Keep humans on high-value judgment.

Three layers, one persistent runtime

Layer A

Forecast & Backtest

For ideas with a target and enough history. Rolling ŷₜ vs realised y, trigger-based strategies, attribution, failure-mode analysis.

Layer B

Scenario & Simulation

For ideas with no historical analog. Bear / base / bull, Kelly sizing, multi-agent what-if, calibration against prediction markets.

Layer C

Operator (human-in-the-loop)

Shadow → small-live. Automate trades under the threshold, escalate the large ones, validate process — not just PnL.

Research direction underneath: a Financial World Model — an event causal graph, not a market impact model.

Who it's for

Discretionary traders

You have views. You don't want to learn to write code. You want a repeatable, falsifiable way to test them.

Small investment teams

You want compound research on a shared facts layer — the way programmers compound engineering in a shared repo.

Future LPs

You want a clear year-one plan, a defensible positioning, and quarterly reports generated from the research graph itself.
